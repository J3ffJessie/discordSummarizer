# Changelog

## [1.2.0] - 2026-05-13

### New Feature
- Added Top Members section to the dashboard available at https://torc-bot.onrender.com/public/dashboard.html?guildId={serverId} . This will show you the top posters from your community across a span of time. Since this feature is just going live, once the backfill is complete you will have the last 60 days worth of data for members in your community.  Hopefully this provides some insight to your most active members so you can celebrate their support in your community.
